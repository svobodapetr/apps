When you start the game, you have 5 seconds to type the first given word.

After you type the word correctly, press enter to confirm.

For each correctly spelled word you´ll get +1 score point and time bonus.

Time bonus depends on difficulty setting.

Easy: +5s

Medium: +3s

Hard: +2s

WARNING:

If you enter incorrectly written word, you´ll loose 1 score point. If your score is 0, you´ll loose -1s instead.
